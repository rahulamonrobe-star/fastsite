    WE ARE THE
    GOLDEN CROWN LITERARY SOCIETY

    A nonprofit organization supporting an international community of readers, writers, publishers, editors, audiobook narrators, and fans devoted to increasing the diversity, accessibility, quality, and visibility of sapphic and women-loving-women literature.

    Learn More

    Photo Credit: Wildly Connected Photography

    As lovers of literature, we understand the power of words. We appreciate how the right words can provide shades of meaning, nuance, and clarity of expression. The words we use to describe our identity are particularly powerful, giving us language to describe who we are in a world that doesn’t always appreciate the rich diversity within the LGBTQIA+ spectra.

     

    The way we describe ourselves as individuals is myriad (lesbian, bisexual, pansexual, asexual, demisexual, queer, cisgender, transgender, non-binary, Two Spirit, et al) and as we understand more about our identity, personally and collectively, our language expands to incorporate that knowledge.GCLS is, and will always be, fully inclusive of all identities in the sapphic and women-loving women space. We are all welcome. We are all GCLS.
